Privacy Policy – LumoHome Lite

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Summary: LumoHome Lite does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All settings are stored locally on your device only.

1. Introduction

LumoHome Lite ("the App") is a smart home controller application developed by Vladimir Cibukovac ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it on your Android device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device identifiers
  • Any data through third-party analytics services (no Firebase, no Google Analytics)

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information exclusively on your device using Android's local storage (SharedPreferences and SQLite). This data never leaves your device:

  • MQTT broker connection settings (address, port, username, password)
  • Dashboard configuration and device settings
  • Automation rules and schedules
  • Sensor history data
  • App preferences (theme, language, display settings)

4. MQTT Communication

The App communicates directly with your MQTT broker (e.g., your local home server or a cloud broker such as HiveMQ) to control your Tasmota and Zigbee devices. This communication is established solely between your device and your broker — we have no access to this data, nor do we operate any intermediate servers.

The security of this connection (including TLS/SSL encryption) depends on your broker configuration. We recommend using TLS-encrypted connections and strong passwords.

5. Push Notifications

The App can receive push notifications sent directly from your Tasmota/Zigbee bridge via the ntfy open-source notification service. These notifications are configured and sent entirely by your own devices — we do not send, intercept, or have access to any notification content.

6. Biometric Authentication

The App may request biometric authentication (fingerprint or face recognition) on supported devices for securing access to certain features. Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's operating system (Android BiometricPrompt API) and is never accessed, stored, or transmitted by the App.

7. Internet Permission

The App requires internet access solely to connect to your MQTT broker. It does not use internet access to contact any external servers operated by us.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
